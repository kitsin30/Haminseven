Our Agent

Androxus

Androxus is a flanking hero. His main task is to harass the enemy team through flanking attacks that destroy the enemy defense. His main tool is a revolver that can fire single shots or burst attacks.

Lex

Lex is a frontline hero. Don't get misled by his small height - this dwarf can survive even the most finely crafted ambush. Lex can put up a shield and defense turrets that can be placed in the location chosen by you

Koga

Koga is a damage dealer, armed with a quick-firing crossbow and supported by Zigs, her companion. Cassie is a part of every strong party. Aside from damage dealing, she has the ability to shove her target away.

Vato

Vatu is a typical assassin. In combat, she makes use of a crossbow, but she also has smoke bombs at her disposal which effectively blind the enemy.

Vora

Vora is a flanking champion. Thanks to his high mobility, survivability, and the ability to deal serious damage, he is able to successfully harass the enemy through flanking attacks.
